MentorX Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 29, 2025

MentorX ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website MentorX.com (or any subdomain thereof), use our applications, and interact with our services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Services, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities on the Services, or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the Services, the choices you make, and the products and features you use. The personal information we may collect includes:

  • Identity Data: Your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile Data: Information you provide for your mentor or mentee profile, such as educational background, professional experience, skills, interests, and profile picture.
  • Communication Data: Content of messages and communications exchanged between users (mentors, mentees, and administrators) on the platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our Services, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, and access times.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Maintain Services: To operate and deliver the MentorX platform, enable mentor-mentee connections, and facilitate communication.
  • To Personalize User Experience: To tailor content, mentorship recommendations, and learning paths based on your profile and interactions.
  • To Improve Our Services: To understand how users interact with the platform, identify areas for improvement, and develop new features.
  • For Communication: To send you service-related notifications, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect the integrity and security of our Services and to detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Limited profile information (e.g., name, skills, experience, and public profile data) will be visible to other users (mentors or mentees) to facilitate connections.
  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., hosting, data analysis, email delivery).
  • For Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of our business.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@mentorx.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently.
